Ingredients:

 1 lb ground beef
 1 package taco seasoning
 1/2 cup water
 1 small package flour tortillias
 1 cup shreded cheese (your choice)

Directions:
1. brown ground beef over med heat
2. drain beef
3. add package taco seasining and 1/2 cup water
4. bring to simmer, then simmer 5 min.
5. serve on flour tortillias and ad cheese
